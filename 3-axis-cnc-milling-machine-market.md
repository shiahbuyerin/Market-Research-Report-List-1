<p><p>The 3-axis CNC milling machine market is categorized into three types: horizontal, vertical, and universal. Horizontal CNC mills have a horizontal spindle orientation, allowing for efficient side milling and higher stability for larger workpieces. Vertical CNC mills feature a vertical spindle, providing versatility and precision for complex part machining. Universal CNC mills combine both horizontal and vertical capabilities, offering enhanced flexibility for various applications, making them suitable for diverse manufacturing needs and complex geometries.</p></p>

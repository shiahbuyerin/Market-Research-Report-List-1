<p><ul><li>Single Card</li><li>Dual Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G vehicle router market is categorized into single card and dual card types. Single card routers use one cellular module to connect to 5G networks, offering a cost-effective solution for basic connectivity needs. Dual card routers, on the other hand, incorporate two cellular modules, enabling simultaneous connections to multiple networks, enhancing redundancy and failover capabilities. This dual configuration ensures better performance, higher bandwidth, and improved reliability, making it ideal for mission-critical applications in vehicles.</p></p>

<p><p>Adhesive-bonded Cross-laminated Timber (CLT) is an innovative engineered wood product made by layering multiple sheets of lumber in alternating directions, bonded with adhesives. The construction industry’s shift towards prefabricated solutions has highlighted the benefits of CLT, including cost-effectiveness and reduced assembly time.

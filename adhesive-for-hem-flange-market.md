<p><p>The adhesive for hem flange market is divided into two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready to use and cure through exposure to moisture or heat, offering convenience for users. In contrast, two-component adhesives require the mixing of a resin and a hardener before application, providing enhanced strength and durability. Depending on the application needs, manufacturers choose between these types based on factors like curing time, bonding strength, and environmental resistance.</p></p>

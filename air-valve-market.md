<p><strong>The Air Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Air Valves</li><li>Double Air Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air valve market is primarily segmented into single air valves and double air valves. Single air valves allow for the release of air from pipeline systems, enhancing efficiency and preventing air-related issues. They are typically used in smaller applications. Conversely, double air valves serve a dual purpose: they release air during filling and allow the inflow of air during draining, making them suitable for larger systems. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal pipeline performance and preventing pressure imbalances.</p></p>

<p><p>The Fiber Optic Current Sensor (AFOCS) market comprises two primary types: AC and DC sensors. AC sensors are designed to measure alternating current, utilizing optical technology to ensure high accuracy and immunity to electromagnetic interference. They are prevalent in applications like power distribution and monitoring. DC sensors, on the other hand, measure direct current and are essential for applications in renewable energy systems and battery management. Both types leverage fiber optic technology for enhanced performance and reliability in various sectors.</p></p>

<p><p>The arc welding torch market is primarily divided into two types: air-cooled and water-cooled welding torches. Air-cooled torches are designed for lighter-duty applications, using ambient air to dissipate heat, making them portable and easy to maintain. Conversely, water-cooled torches are built for higher-duty tasks, utilizing circulating water to manage heat effectively, which allows for prolonged use without overheating. This differentiation caters to various industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and performance across different welding environments.</p></p>

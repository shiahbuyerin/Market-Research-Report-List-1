<p><p>The automobile hitch ball market comprises three primary types: gooseneck hitch balls, pintle hitch balls, and trailer hitch balls. Gooseneck hitch balls are designed for heavy-duty towing, typically mounted in the bed of a truck, offering a more stable connection for larger trailers. Pintle hitch balls are ideal for rugged applications and provide a secure attachment for military and construction vehicles. Trailer hitch balls are the most common, used for a wide range of recreational trailers and are versatile for various towing needs.</p></p>

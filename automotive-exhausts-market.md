<p><strong>The Automotive Exhausts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li><li>Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF)</li><li>Lean Nox Trap (LNT)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive exhaust market includes several key components designed to reduce emissions. The Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC) converts harmful gases into less harmful substances, primarily addressing carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons. The Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) captures and stores particulate matter from diesel exhaust. The Lean Nox Trap (LNT) reduces nitrogen oxides by adsorbing them during lean operation. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) utilizes a urea-based solution to transform nitrogen oxides into nitrogen and water, effectively reducing pollution in diesel engines.</p></p>

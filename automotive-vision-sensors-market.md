<p><strong>The Automotive Vision Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocular Vision</li><li>Binocular (stereo) Vision</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive vision sensors market comprises primarily two types: monocular vision and binocular (stereo) vision systems. Monocular vision utilizes a single camera to capture images and analyze the environment, offering a cost-effective solution for tasks like lane detection and object recognition. In contrast, binocular vision employs two cameras to create depth perception by simulating human binocular vision, enhancing object localization and spatial awareness. These technologies are essential for advancing driver assistance systems and autonomous vehicle capabilities, promoting safety and efficiency.</p></p>

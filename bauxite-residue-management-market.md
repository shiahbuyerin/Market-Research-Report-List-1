<p><strong>The Bauxite Residue Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Stacking</li><li>Wet Storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bauxite residue management encompasses two primary market types: dry stacking and wet storage. Dry stacking involves dewatering bauxite residue and stacking it in compact, engineered facilities, minimizing environmental impact and land use. This method enhances long-term stability and reduces leachate risks. Conversely, wet storage maintains bauxite residue in liquid form within retention dams. While it can be easier to manage initially, it poses risks of overflow and environmental contamination. Both methods address the challenges of safely storing and managing bauxite byproducts.</p></p>

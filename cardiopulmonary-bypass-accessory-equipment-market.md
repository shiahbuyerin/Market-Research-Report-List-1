<p><p>The cardiopulmonary bypass accessory equipment market includes various types of roller pumps essential for heart surgeries. Single roller pumps utilize one set of rollers to propel blood, providing a simpler and more compact design. In contrast, double roller pumps use two sets of rollers, offering enhanced flow rates and precise control over blood delivery. Both types are crucial for maintaining blood circulation and oxygenation during procedures, with their choice often depending on the specific requirements of the surgical environment.</p></p>

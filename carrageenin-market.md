<p><strong>The Carrageenin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kappa-Carrageenin</li><li>Iota-Carrageenin</li><li>Lambda Carrageenin</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carrageenan market is categorized into Kappa, Iota, Lambda, and Other types. Kappa-carrageenan, derived from red seaweed, forms a strong gel upon cooling, making it ideal for dairy and meat applications. Iota-carrageenan creates softer gels, suitable for desserts and sauces, as it reacts with calcium. Lambda-carrageenan remains soluble, enhancing viscosity without gel formation, often used in dairy products. Other varieties include blends and modified forms, catering to diverse food, cosmetic, and pharmaceutical industries while meeting various functional requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>Cloud-based tax software is available primarily through two market types: perpetual licenses and SaaS licenses. Perpetual licenses involve a one-time purchase, allowing users to own the software indefinitely, but often requiring additional costs for updates and support. In contrast, SaaS (Software as a Service) licenses operate on a subscription model, providing continuous access to the software through the cloud. This model offers regular updates and support without the need for significant upfront investments, catering to diverse user needs and budgets.</p></p>

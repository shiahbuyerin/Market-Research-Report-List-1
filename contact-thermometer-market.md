<p><p>The contact thermometer market is primarily segmented into two types based on sensing technology: thermistor sensors and thermocouple sensors. Contact thermometers with thermistor sensors offer high sensitivity and precision at lower temperature ranges, making them ideal for applications like medical diagnostics and food processing. In contrast, contact thermometers with thermocouple sensors can measure a broader temperature range and are durable, often used in industrial settings where extreme conditions are prevalent, ensuring accurate temperature measurements for various applications.</p></p>

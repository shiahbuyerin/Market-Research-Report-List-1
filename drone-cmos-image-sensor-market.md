<p><p>The Drone CMOS Image Sensor market includes three key types: Monochrome, RGB, and RGB-NIR. Monochrome sensors capture high-resolution grayscale images, making them ideal for applications requiring precision in low light. RGB sensors, featuring red, green, and blue filters, are optimized for vibrant color imaging, widely used in photography and videography. RGB-NIR sensors combine RGB capabilities with near-infrared (NIR) sensitivity, enabling enhanced imaging for agricultural, surveillance, and environmental monitoring applications, where analyzing both visible and NIR spectra is crucial.</p></p>

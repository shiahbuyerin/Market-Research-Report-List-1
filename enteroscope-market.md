<p><p>The enteroscope market is categorized into two primary types: video endoscopes and fiberscopes. Video endoscopes utilize advanced imaging technology to provide high-resolution, real-time visuals of the gastrointestinal tract, enhancing diagnostic precision. In contrast, fiberscopes rely on flexible fiber optic technology, offering a more traditional method for visualization but often with lower image quality. Both types play crucial roles in gastrointestinal procedures, catering to different clinical needs and preferences, ultimately improving patient outcomes and enhancing procedural effectiveness.</p></p>

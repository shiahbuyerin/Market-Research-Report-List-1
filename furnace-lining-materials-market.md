<p><p>The furnace lining materials market is categorized into three types based on their chemical properties. </p><p>**Acidic Type** materials, such as silica and alumina, are used in environments where acidic slags are present, offering excellent resistance to corrosion. </p><p>**Basic Type** materials, such as magnesia and dolomite, withstand basic slags and higher temperatures, making them suitable for non-ferrous and some ferrous metal processing. </p><p>**Neutral Type** materials, like chromite and certain refractories, provide balanced characteristics, functioning effectively in both acidic and basic conditions.</p></p>

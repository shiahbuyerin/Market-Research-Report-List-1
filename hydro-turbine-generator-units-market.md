<p><p>Hydro turbine generator units are essential in hydroelectric power generation, categorized mainly into Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. Francis turbines, suited for medium head applications, use a radial inflow design. Kaplan turbines, designed for low head sites, utilize adjustable blades for efficiency. Pelton turbines excel in high head conditions, employing impulse principles. Other types include crossflow and bulb turbines, which cater to specific site requirements. Each type optimizes energy conversion based on water flow and site characteristics.</p></p>

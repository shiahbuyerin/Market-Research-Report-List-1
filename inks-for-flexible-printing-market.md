<p><strong>The Inks for Flexible Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based Inks</li><li>Solvent-Based Inks</li><li>UV-Cured Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible printing inks market comprises three primary types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks use water as a solvent, offering low environmental impact and excellent print quality, ideal for packaging. Solvent-based inks, containing organic solvents, provide strong adhesion and durability but raise environmental concerns. UV-cured inks utilize ultraviolet light to cure the ink instantly, delivering vibrant colors and resistance to abrasion, making them suitable for high-speed printing applications. Each ink type serves distinct needs within flexible printing.</p></p>

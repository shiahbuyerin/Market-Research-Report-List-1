<p><p>The Lead Frames market can be categorized into two main types based on the manufacturing process: Stamping Process Lead Frames and Etching Process Lead Frames. Stamping Process Lead Frames involve the mechanical punching or pressing of metal sheets to create precise shapes, offering high production efficiency and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, Etching Process Lead Frames utilize chemical solutions to remove material and achieve intricate designs, allowing for more complex patterns and finer detail. Both methods cater to diverse application needs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>

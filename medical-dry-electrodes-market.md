<p><p>The medical dry electrodes market is categorized into two primary types: disposable and reusable electrodes. Disposable electrodes are designed for single-use, offering convenience and reducing the risk of cross-contamination, making them ideal for short-term monitoring or procedures. Reusable electrodes, on the other hand, are durable and can be cleaned and sterilized for multiple uses, providing cost-effectiveness and sustainability over time. Both types are essential for applications such as electromyography and electrocardiography, ensuring effective patient monitoring and diagnostics.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Needle-Free Injection Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Injections</li><li>Liquid Injections</li><li>Depot or Projectile Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needle-free injection device market is categorized into three main types: powder injections, liquid injections, and depot or projectile injections. Powder injections utilize high-pressure gas to deliver solid drug particles through the skin, offering quick drug absorption. Liquid injections, on the other hand, use a fluid formulation that is propelled at high speeds, allowing for precise delivery of medications. Depot or projectile injections are designed for long-lasting drug release by delivering larger volumes of medication deep into tissues, ensuring sustained therapeutic effects.</p></p>

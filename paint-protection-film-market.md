<p><strong>The Paint Protection Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVC Type</li><li>PU Type</li><li>TPU Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Paint Protection Film (PPF) market consists of three main types: PVC, PU, and TPU. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is cost-effective but less flexible and durable. PU (Polyurethane) offers enhanced clarity, flexibility, and UV resistance, making it ideal for high-end applications. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) combines the best features of PVC and PU, providing superior abrasion resistance and self-healing properties. Each type caters to different customer needs, balancing cost, performance, and durability in vehicle protection solutions.</p></p>

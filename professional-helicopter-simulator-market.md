<p><strong>The Professional Helicopter Simulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FFS</li><li>ATD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The professional helicopter simulator market consists of two primary types: Full Flight Simulators (FFS) and Aviation Training Devices (ATD). FFS are high-fidelity systems that provide realistic flight experiences, including motion and environmental factors, ideal for proficiency training and assessments. In contrast, ATDs focus on specific training elements, often lacking full motion capabilities, but are essential for procedural practice and skill development. Both play vital roles in ensuring safety and competency in helicopter operations, catering to varying training needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>

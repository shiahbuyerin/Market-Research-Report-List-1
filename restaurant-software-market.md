<p><ul><li>Traditional on-Premise</li><li>Cloud (SaaS Delivery Model)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The restaurant software market encompasses two primary types: Traditional on-premise solutions and Cloud (SaaS) delivery models. Traditional on-premise software involves installing applications directly on local servers, offering greater control but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Conversely, Cloud-based solutions utilize a SaaS model, allowing restaurants to access software via the internet, reducing installation costs and simplifying updates. Cloud systems provide flexibility, scalability, and remote accessibility, making them increasingly popular among restaurateurs seeking efficient management tools.</p></p>

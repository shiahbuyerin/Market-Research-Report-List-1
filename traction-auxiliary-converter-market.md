<p><p>The Traction Auxiliary Converter market is divided into two main types: integrated and detached converters. Integrated converters are built within the traction system, providing a compact solution that minimizes space and weight. These converters enhance overall system efficiency and simplify design. On the other hand, detached converters operate independently, allowing for easier maintenance and flexibility in installation. This separation can improve thermal management and enable the use of more powerful components, catering to diverse operational needs in transit vehicles.</p></p>

<p><p>The Virtual Dissection Table market is categorized into two main types: rotatable and non-rotatable. Rotatable tables allow users to manipulate the view of the specimen from multiple angles, enhancing the learning experience and providing a comprehensive understanding of anatomy. In contrast, non-rotatable tables offer fixed perspectives, focusing on specific views without the added flexibility. Both types serve educational purposes in medical training and research, but rotatable tables typically provide a more interactive and immersive experience for users.</p></p>
